History of the College of Science

Traced back to 1984, the College of Science stands as a pillar of academic excellence and innovation within the University of Rizal System.


Foundational Years (1984–2005)

Formerly known as the Institute of Arts and Sciences, the college began its journey on March 23, 1984. It was established under the authority of MECS Order No. 16, s. 1984.

During these two decades, the Institute offered four foundational programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Biology
  • Bachelor of Science in Psychology
  • Bachelor of Science in Mathematics
  • Bachelor of Science in Guidance and Counseling

The Institute Becomes a College

In 2005, the Institute of Arts and Sciences was formally renamed the College of Science, reflecting its evolving identity and expanding academic scope.

MECS Order No. 16, s. 1984

Dated March 23, 1984

"The Institute began its journey by offering four foundational programs... nurturing generations of scholars and professionals."

Curricular Expansion

To meet technological demands, BS Mathematics major in Computer Science was introduced via Board Resolution No. 04-11-97 on September 27, 1997.

A major milestone was reached in 2021, when the standalone Bachelor of Science in Computer Science was launched through BOR Resolution No. 02.
